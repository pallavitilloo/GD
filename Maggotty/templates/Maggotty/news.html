Celebrating School Day

Students came together on a day without classes to celebrate School Day. Under a beautiful autumn sky on the Student Center Quad, students enjoyed ice cream, created with sidewalk chalk, danced, played games and showed off their School Pride, a one-day break that brought the campus together – outdoors and socially distanced – during a fall semester like no other.

A Transformed School Hall Reopens

Starting October 13, the building began hosting Maggotty High Central 1-Stop, the School's one-stop student services center for all current and prospective students. Maggotty High Central is located on the 2nd floor main entrance. Students are encouraged to stop by for assistance and support concerning registration, billing, financial aid or any other questions.
