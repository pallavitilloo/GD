MHAA 2013 Dinner Dance

December 7, 2013
Richie Rich Place and Banquet Hall
110-19 Atlantic Ave. Queens, NY 11418

The event held at Richie Rick Place and Banquet Hall was held on December 7, 2013. Present at this meeting were Veronica Blake-DeWalt, Uriah Chambers, Allison Johnson-Russel, Hubert Johnson, Clarence Jones, Orrett McNeil, Sheila McCormack-Vanenburg, Beverkyn Smith, Bradley Thompson, Kie Wright. Between June 2009 and May 2011 sporadic meetings were held at the Mona Prep Academy at South Conduit Ave, Roachdale, Queens. In early 2011 the Association was joined by such Alumni as Valrie Samuels, Homer Duhaney, Elton Hanson, Lorane Phillips, and Olive Brown, all of whom breathed new life into the Association.
